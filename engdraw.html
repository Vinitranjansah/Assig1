INDIAN INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY,SHIBPUR

B.Tech , 2st SEMESTER EXAMINATION ,JULY 2023

ENGINEERING DRAWING

Full Marks :70 Time : 180 minutes

Answer any five questions. All questions carry equal marks.

  1. Write the steps and draw the orthographic projection of a cube.
  2. Write the difference between isometric and oblique projection.
  3. Write the rules and draw the dimensioning of a simple object.
  4. Write the types and draw the examples of section views.
  5. Write the meaning and draw the symbols of common engineering features such as threads, holes, chamfers, etc.
  6. Write the advantages and disadvantages of computer-aided design (CAD).
  7. Write the applications and limitations of engineering drawing.
