INDIAN INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY,SHIBPUR

B.Tech , 2st SEMESTER EXAMINATION ,JULY 2023

MECHANICS

Full Marks :70 Time : 180 minutes

Answer any five questions. All questions carry equal marks.

  1. Write the definition and the formula of Newton's second law of motion.
  2. Write the difference between scalar and vector quantities.
  3. Write the types and draw the examples of forces such as friction, tension, normal, etc.
  4. Write the conservation of energy principle and apply it to a simple system.
  5. Write the definition and the formula of torque.
  6. Write the types and draw the examples of simple machines such as lever, pulley, wedge, etc.
  7. Write the applications and challenges of mechanics.
