B.Tech , 1st SEMESTER EXAMINATION ,JULY 2023

ENVIRONMENT AND ECOLOGY

Full Marks :70 Time : 180 minutes

Answer any five questions. All questions carry equal marks.

  1. Write the definition and the examples of biotic and abiotic factors in an ecosystem.
  2. Write the difference between food chain and food web.
  3. Write the types and draw the examples of symbiotic relationships such as mutualism, commensalism, parasitism, etc.
  4. Write the causes and effects of global warming and climate change.
  5. Write the definition and the examples of renewable and non-renewable energy sources.
  6. Write the methods and apply them to reduce, reuse and recycle waste materials.
  7. Write the importance and challenges of environmental conservation and sustainability.
